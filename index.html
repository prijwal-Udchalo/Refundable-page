What Is Refundable Program

The Refundable Program allows customers to apply for a 100% refund of cancellation of flight tickets due to an unforeseen and/or emergency circumstance highlighted in the terms of use*. udChalo Customers can add Refundable Program at the time of booking a flight and make their bookings 100% refundable*.

How to apply for a Refund

If your ticket is covered under Refundable Program, follow the 4 step process to get a full refund.

1

Go to Manage Bookings and cancel the flight

2

Check your eligibility under the Refundable Terms

3

Submit the cancellation form with your valid email ID and supporting documents

4

Post-verification from Refundable Team, the amount will be credited in your bank account.

FAQs

Refundable Program Terms are designed to upgrade the standard refund terms of the customer’s booking to allow them to apply for a 100% refund should they not be able to attend due to an unforeseen and/or emergency circumstance.
If the customer is affected by an unforeseen circumstance included in the Refundable Program Terms, they will be entitled to apply for a 100% refund directly to their chosen bank account through a simple online Refund Application Form. This can be any time up to the time of your departure and even 60 days after the date of departure.
